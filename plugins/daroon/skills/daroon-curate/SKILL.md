# Daroon Curate

Curate Daroon when captured context has become stable, accepted, and useful enough to belong in maintained pages. Curation keeps the shared knowledge base valuable to both the user and future agents by turning raw continuity and sources into concise, source-aware knowledge.

## Workspace

Default Daroon workspace root: `~/.daroon/current`.

Maintain canonical pages under `projects/`, `people/`, `topics/`, and the routing brief `DAROON.md` inside that workspace unless the user gives a more specific path.

## Default Behavior

- Promote only accepted or strongly evidenced context.
- Keep canon concise.
- Preserve the distinction between current maintained pages and raw logs.
- Update routing pointers when a page becomes important for future work.
- Avoid speculative personality or project conclusions.

## Canon Targets

- `projects/` for ongoing work and product direction.
- `people/` for durable relationship context.
- `topics/` for cross-project concepts, preferences, and operating principles.
- `DAROON.md` for active pointers only.
